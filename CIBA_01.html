Our Story

About CIBA

Rooted in truth. Committed to excellence. Devoted to the glory of God.

Church of I AM Bible Academy (CIBA) is a Bible learning center established under a registered religious trust in accordance with the Indian Trust Act. Located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, the academy was founded with a singular, God-honoring vision: to produce a generation of doctrinally sound, spiritually mature, and ministry-ready believers equipped for every good work in the Kingdom of God.

CIBA operates on the foundational conviction that the Word of God is the sole, sufficient, and supreme authority for all of faith and practice. Our programs are designed not merely to impart academic knowledge, but to transform hearts, sharpen minds, and cultivate servants who will faithfully proclaim the full counsel of God.

Our Vision

To become a premier, nationally recognized theological institution that shapes doctrinally sound, Christ-centered leaders capable of transforming the church and society of India through the faithful proclamation of the Gospel.

We envision a Telugu-speaking church that is deeply rooted in the Scriptures, equipped to face contemporary challenges with biblical wisdom, and passionate about making disciples of all nations.

Our Mission

To proclaim the full counsel of God through structured, rigorous, and accessible biblical education — training pastors, elders, church planters, and lay believers in sound doctrine, biblical exegesis, and practical ministry.

We are committed to providing high-quality theological training in the Telugu language, removing barriers of access so that every believer may grow in the grace and knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Motto

"Biblical Precision and Spiritual Prominence"

These twin pillars define the character of every CIBA graduate. We refuse to separate intellectual rigor from spiritual devotion — true theology that does not inflame the heart toward Christ is mere academic exercise.

Our Slogan

"Scripture is the Interpreter of Scripture"

This time-honored Reformation principle — Scriptura Scripturae Interpres — governs our entire hermeneutical and theological method. We believe the best commentary on the Bible is the Bible itself, and we train our students to interpret every passage in light of the whole counsel of God's Word.

Legal & Institutional Details

Church of I AM Bible Academy operates under a formally registered religious trust in India, in accordance with the provisions of the Indian Trusts Act. This registration ensures full legal accountability, institutional transparency, and the ability to operate as a recognized ecclesiastical and educational body within the Republic of India.

Our trust registration enables us to issue recognized certifications upon course completion, receive donations for ministry purposes, organize official seminars and conferences, and partner with other registered church bodies and Christian organizations across the nation.

Doctrinal Framework

Our Theology

Standing on the Five Solas of the Reformation and the rich heritage of Reformed, biblical Christianity.

Sola Scriptura

Scripture Alone

The Bible alone is the inspired, infallible, inerrant Word of God — the sole, sufficient, supreme authority for all matters of faith and practice.

  • Biblical Inerrancy & Infallibility
  • The Canon of Scripture
  • Hermeneutics & Interpretation
  • Scripture vs. Tradition
  • Verbal Plenary Inspiration

Sola Fide

Faith Alone

Justification is received through faith alone — not by works, merit, or sacraments — a faith that is itself the gift of God's sovereign grace.

  • Justification by Faith
  • Imputed Righteousness
  • The Nature of Saving Faith
  • Faith vs. Works
  • The Reformation Controversy

Sola Gratia

Grace Alone

Salvation is entirely of God's sovereign grace — from election to glorification. Man contributes nothing to his redemption; all is of God.

  • Total Depravity
  • Unconditional Election
  • Irresistible Grace
  • Perseverance of the Saints
  • Monergism vs. Synergism

Solus Christus

Christ Alone

Jesus Christ is the only Mediator between God and man. Salvation is found in no other — not in Mary, saints, priests, or any human institution.

  • The Hypostatic Union
  • Penal Substitutionary Atonement
  • The High Priestly Ministry
  • Christ as Prophet, Priest, King
  • The Resurrection & Ascension

Soli Deo Gloria

To God Alone be Glory

All of life, worship, and ministry must be directed to the glory of God alone. Man's chief end is to glorify God and enjoy Him forever.

  • The Sovereignty of God
  • Doxological Theology
  • Regulative Principle of Worship
  • Vocation & Cultural Mandate
  • Eschatology & God's Glory
